该业务流程始于“Start Event”，由“Secretary”角色的“Check Shipping Amount”任务开启。根据“Shipping Amount”情况，流程分为不同路径。若金额小，走“Normal post shipment”；若金额大，获取“Shipping Company Offer”后，经“Logistic Co 1”“Logistic Co 2”“Logistic Co 3”，由“Secretary”选择“Shipping Company”。然后，根据“Normal post shipment”或“Choose Shipping Company”结果，经“Create Package Label”“Any Insurance”（若需则“Prepare Insurance”），“Warehouseman”“Package the Goods”，“Logistic Department Head”“Prepare Insurance”（若需），最后“Logistic Company”“Package pick - up”，流程结束。
