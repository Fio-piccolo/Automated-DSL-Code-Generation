The requirements are as follows: In the context of Direct Debit Transaction Information ('DirectDebitTransactionInformation1'), only one instance of Private Identification for the Ultimate Creditor ('ultmtCdtr Id prvtId') may be present. The 'Ultimate Creditor' is either undefined or the size of the 'private identification' of 'Ultimate Creditor' must be less than or equal to one. This is based on the SEPA B2B Direct Debit C2B Implementation Guidelines Version 1.3, specifically referenced to row 12 on page 12.
