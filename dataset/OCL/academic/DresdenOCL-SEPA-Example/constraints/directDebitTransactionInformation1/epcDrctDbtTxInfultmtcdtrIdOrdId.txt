The requirements are as follows: 

In the Direct Debit Transaction Information (DirectDebitTransactionInformation1), for the Ultimate Creditor Organisation Identification (ultmtCdtr.id.orgId), it can only contain one of the following sub-elements: 

- BIC (Bank Identifier Code) 
- IBEI (International Business Entity Identifier) 
- BEI (Business Entity Identifier) 
- EANGLN (Global Location Number) 
- USCHU (US DUNS Number) 
- DUNS (Data Universal Numbering System) 
- BkPtyId (Bank Party Identifier)
- TaxIdNb (Tax Identification Number) 
- PrtryId (Proprietary Identification)

This is according to the row 11 on page 12 of the SEPA B2B Direct Debit C2B Implementation Guidelines Version 1.3.
