The requirement is as follows:

1. All the authors of any student paper must have at least one author who is a student.

2. At least one paper out of all papers must be a student paper. And the total number of student papers cannot exceed 4.

3. The word count for all papers must be less than 10,000.

4. No researcher can review their own submission.

5. For all papers, their assigned referees must not include any students.
